Shortfall — App. C (°C)

= Test CWT − Predicted CWT from Cross Plot 2 (at test WBT).
Positive = underperforming. Negative = exceeds spec.

CW Deviation — App. M (°C)

= CWT at Design Conditions − Design CWT. Normalises test KaV/L to design WBT & flow — direct contract deviation. Positive = below spec.

Capability (%)

= (Adj. Flow ÷ Pred. Flow at Test CWT) × 100.
≥ 100% = spec met. < 100% = failing. Per ATC-105 App. C.
